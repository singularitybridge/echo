Key Findings

WAN 2.1 FLF2V

98% frame matching rate with fastest generation time. Excellent balance of speed, quality, and frame accuracy. Recommended for production use.

Veo 3.1 Fast

Good quality and fast generation, but does not guarantee end frame accuracy. Best for general video generation, not ideal for precise scene transitions.

Vidu Q1

Solid quality with moderate generation speed. Good alternative when WAN 2.1 is unavailable, though slower than WAN 2.1.

Framepack FLF2V

Most affordable option at $0.033/sec, but significantly slower generation time (6x slower than WAN 2.1). Best for budget-conscious projects where time is not critical.

Recommended Model

WAN 2.1 FLF2V

For transition scenes requiring precise frame-to-frame continuity, WAN 2.1 FLF2V is the clear winner with its 98% frame matching rate, fastest generation time (38.9s), and reasonable pricing at $0.40/gen.
