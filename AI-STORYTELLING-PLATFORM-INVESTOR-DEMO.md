## üé¨ Production Strategy

### Character Design

**Persona**: Modern content creator / marketing professional

**Visual Style**:
- Professional but approachable
- Modern tech workspace setting
- Age 25-40 demographic
- Gender-neutral appeal
- Casual business attire

**Why This Choice**:
- Represents target customer
- Aspirational but relatable
- Works across all 8 scenes
- Neutral enough for broad appeal

### Visual Consistency Guidelines

**CRITICAL**: Follow the same rules as Factory of AI Agents:

- Use **"person"** in prompts, NOT character names
- Focus on **actions and emotions**, not appearance
- Avoid visual effects that could change character look
- Let reference images define appearance
- Prompts direct performance only

**Example Prompts**:
- ‚úÖ CORRECT: "Person sits at desk, looks frustrated, checks watch"
- ‚ùå WRONG: "Alex the content creator with glasses looks frustrated"

- **Model**: Veo 3.1 (latest, best quality)
- **Resolution**: 720p (fast generation, good quality)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (mobile-first, social media optimized)
- **Duration**: 8 seconds per scene (standard for short-form)
