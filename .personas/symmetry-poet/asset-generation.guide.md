# Asset Generation Guide: Symmetry Poet

## Visual Identity
Generate assets with perfect symmetry, pastel color palettes, and whimsical storybook aesthetics that feel handcrafted and nostalgic.

## Character Design Principles

### Physical Characteristics
- **Stylized but grounded**: Slight exaggeration while maintaining realism
- **Distinctive features**: Unique faces, expressive eyes, strong silhouettes
- **Period-ambiguous**: Could be 1960s-1980s, timeless quality
- **Quirky details**: Freckles, unique hairstyles, memorable features
- **Age variety**: Children, adults, elderly - all with charm

### Wardrobe & Styling
- **Vintage aesthetics**: 1960s-1970s fashion sensibilities
- **Pastel colors**: Soft pink, powder blue, mint green, pale yellow
- **Patterns**: Stripes, polka dots, plaids, vintage florals
- **Accessories**: Thick-rimmed glasses, berets, bow ties, vintage jewelry
- **Hair**: Precise cuts, bobs, side parts, distinctive styles
- **Footwear**: Loafers, mary janes, vintage sneakers

### Color Palette for Characters
- Pastel primaries: Soft pink, baby blue, mint green
- Secondary pastels: Lavender, peach, lemon yellow
- Neutrals: Cream, beige, soft gray
- Skin tones: Warm and peachy
- Hair: Blonde, auburn, silver, distinctive colors

## Environment & Background Design

### Settings
- **Whimsical interiors**: Perfectly arranged rooms, vintage furniture
- **Storybook exteriors**: Quaint towns, colorful houses, manicured gardens
- **Institutional spaces**: Schools, libraries, hotels with character
- **Natural settings**: Organized nature, geometric trees, structured landscapes
- **Transportation**: Vintage trains, buses, boats with personality

### Lighting Style
- Soft, even lighting with no harsh shadows
- Bright but not overexposed
- Warm color temperature
- Diffused sunlight
- Practical light sources visible and decorative
- Magical golden hour quality

## Reference Image Generation

### Portrait References
- **Framing**: Perfectly centered, symmetrical composition
- **Expression**: Deadpan, slight smile, earnest
- **Lighting**: Soft, frontal, even
- **Background**: Solid pastel color or symmetrical environment
- **Style**: Illustrated quality while maintaining photo-realism

### Landscape References
- **Composition**: Absolute symmetry, centered subjects
- **Depth**: Flat, planimetric, layered like a diorama
- **Color**: Coordinated pastel palette throughout
- **Details**: Every element carefully placed
- **Perspective**: Often overhead or straight-on

## Props & Objects
- Vintage suitcases and trunks
- Retro telephones and radios
- Handwritten letters and notes
- Maps and diagrams
- Vintage cameras and projectors
- Board games and toys
- Books with distinct covers
- Antique globes
- Musical instruments
- Model ships/trains/planes

## Artistic References
- Wes Anderson film frames
- Children's book illustrations (1960s-1970s)
- Vintage travel posters
- Mid-century graphic design
- Norman Rockwell paintings (but more stylized)
- Vintage fashion photography
- Stop-motion animation aesthetics

## Technical Specifications

### For Fal.ai Image Generation
```
Style keywords:
"Wes Anderson aesthetic, symmetrical composition, pastel color palette,
whimsical, storybook illustration, vintage 1970s, centered framing,
flat depth, handcrafted quality, nostalgic"

Negative prompt:
"asymmetrical, dark colors, modern, gritty, realistic lighting,
shallow depth of field, candid, documentary style, harsh shadows"

Quality settings:
- Perfect symmetry in composition
- Pastel color grading
- Even lighting throughout
- Flat perspective
- Detailed textures (fabric, wood, paper)
```

### Character Reference Consistency
- Exact same character design across all references
- Consistent wardrobe and color scheme
- Same hairstyle and accessories
- Centered, symmetrical framing
- Matching pastel background colors

## Mood & Atmosphere
- Whimsical yet melancholic
- Nostalgic and timeless
- Meticulously crafted
- Storybook magical
- Charming with underlying sadness
- Perfectly imperfect

## Environmental Details
- Perfect symmetry in all compositions
- Color-coordinated elements
- Vintage props and set dressing
- Handmade quality to textures
- Geometric arrangements
- Centered subjects
- Overhead "diorama" views

## Typography & Graphics
When including text elements:
- Hand-lettered or vintage typefaces
- Centered and symmetrical
- Pastel or muted ink colors
- Title cards and chapter headings
- Handwritten notes and letters
- Vintage signage

## Patterns & Textures
- Vintage wallpapers (geometric or floral)
- Fabric textures (tweed, corduroy, linen)
- Wood grain (painted or natural)
- Tile patterns (bathroom/kitchen)
- Carpet designs (geometric)
- Aged paper and labels

## What to Avoid
- Asymmetrical compositions
- Dark or saturated colors
- Modern aesthetics
- Candid, documentary style
- Harsh lighting or dramatic shadows
- Depth of field effects
- Gritty realism
- Contemporary fashion or technology
- Disorder or chaos in framing
