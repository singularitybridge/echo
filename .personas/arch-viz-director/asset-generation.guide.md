# Asset Generation Guide: Architecture Viz Director

## Visual Identity

## Core Aesthetic

### Architectural Photography Style
- **Editorial quality**: Magazine-worthy compositions
- **Material honesty**: Real textures, authentic surfaces
- **Light-driven**: Time of day defines mood
- **Human scale**: People ground the architecture
- **Contextual**: Buildings in their environment

### Color Palette

#### Primary Tones
- **Concrete Gray** (#808080 - #A0A0A0): Raw, honest, modern
- **Warm Wood** (#8B7355 - #C4A574): Natural warmth, craft
- **Glass Blue** (#87CEEB - #4A90D9): Sky reflections, transparency
- **Steel Silver** (#C0C0C0 - #D4D4D4): Precision, modernity
- **White Plaster** (#F5F5F5 - #FFFFFF): Light, space, cleanliness

#### Time-of-Day Colors
- **Golden Hour**: Warm oranges, long shadows, #D4A574 warmth
- **Blue Hour**: Deep blues, interior glow, #1E3A5F to #7FB3D3
- **Midday**: Clean whites, soft shadows, neutral tones
- **Morning**: Soft pinks, fresh light, #FFF5E6 warmth

#### Accent Colors
- **Sky Blue** (#87CEEB): Views, openness, aspiration
- **Green Landscape** (#4A7C4E): Vegetation, sustainability
- **Sunset Orange** (#E67E22): Golden hour drama
- **Night Blue** (#1A365D): Evening sophistication

## Character & Human Portrayal

### Human Element
- **Scale reference**: Show how big/small spaces are
- **Silhouettes preferred**: Suggest rather than dominate
- **Natural behavior**: Looking at views, walking, sitting
- **Contemporary dress**: Casual luxury, not suits

### Placement
- At windows, looking out
- Crossing lobbies
- On balconies/terraces
- Seated in living spaces
- Walking toward camera or away

### What to Avoid
- Faces as focus (architecture is the subject)
- Posed, artificial arrangements
- Too many people (cluttered)
- No people at all (scale lost)

## Environment & Architecture Types

### Residential
- **Luxury apartments**: Floor-to-ceiling glass, views, open plan
- **Houses**: Indoor-outdoor flow, gardens, natural light
- **Penthouses**: Terraces, skyline views, double-height spaces

### Commercial
- **Office lobbies**: Double-height, materials, arrival
- **Retail spaces**: Street activation, display, flow
- **Hotels**: Lobby bars, suites, rooftops

### Urban Development
- **Mixed-use**: Ground floor activation, residential above
- **Public spaces**: Plazas, parks, street life
- **Infrastructure**: Bridges, transit, civic buildings

### Institutional
- **Museums**: Large volumes, light, circulation
- **Educational**: Collaborative spaces, natural light
- **Healthcare**: Calming, efficient, humane

## Material Representation

### Concrete
- Show texture and imperfections
- Board-formed patterns
- How it catches light
- Warm in golden hour, cool in shade

### Glass
- Reflections of sky and surroundings
- Transparency showing interior
- Frame sections and mullions
- Clean but not sterile

### Wood
- Grain direction and variation
- Warm tones in any light
- Interior/exterior applications
- Natural aging acceptable

### Metal
- Steel, aluminum, bronze, copper
- Reflections and patina
- Precision and craft
- Contrast with organic materials

### Stone
- Natural variation
- Texture and weight
- Interior and exterior use
- Timeless quality

## Lighting Approach

### Golden Hour (Primary)
- Warm light on facades
- Long dramatic shadows
- Interior/exterior warmth contrast
- Most aspirational mood

### Blue Hour
- Interior lights glowing
- Sky deep blue gradient
- Sophisticated, evening mood
- Pool/water reflections

### Midday
- Clean, professional light
- Soft shadows (overcast preferred)
- Honest representation
- Commercial applications

### Morning
- Fresh, optimistic light
- East-facing facades lit
- New day beginning
- Residential lifestyle

### Night
- Carefully lit architecture
- Interior life visible
- Urban energy
- Dramatic when appropriate

## Compositional Guidelines

### Framing
- **Rule of thirds**: Standard but breakable for symmetry
- **Leading lines**: Use architectural elements
- **Frame within frame**: Doorways, windows, columns
- **Horizon placement**: Low for drama, center for balance

### Perspective
- **Human eye level**: Relatable, inviting
- **Elevated**: Context and layout
- **Ground level**: Drama and monumentality
- **Aerial**: Urban context and planning

### Depth
- **Foreground**: Framing elements, plants, furniture
- **Middle ground**: Main architectural subject
- **Background**: Sky, city, landscape context

## Reference Image Specifications

### Exterior Building Portraits
```
Prompt pattern:
"[Building type] at [time of day], [architectural style], [primary materials],
[context/surroundings], person/people for scale, architectural photography,
35mm lens, [weather conditions], professional quality"

Example:
"Modern residential tower at golden hour, glass and concrete facade,
urban context with park visible, silhouette of person on lower balcony,
architectural photography, 35mm lens, clear sky with warm light"
```

### Interior Spaces
```
Prompt pattern:
"Interior of [space type], [ceiling height] ceiling, [primary materials],
[natural/artificial light source], [furniture style], person at window for scale,
editorial architecture photography, [time of day] light"

Example:
"Interior of open-plan living room, double-height ceiling with floor-to-ceiling
windows, concrete floor and white walls, natural light streaming in,
minimal contemporary furniture, woman standing at window looking at city view,
editorial architecture photography, morning soft light"
```

### Material Details
```
Prompt pattern:
"Close-up of [material] [element], showing [texture/pattern], [light direction],
shallow depth of field, architectural detail photography, tactile quality"

Example:
"Close-up of board-formed concrete wall, showing wood grain texture imprint,
side-lit with warm afternoon light, shallow depth of field, architectural
detail photography, showing craft and materiality"
```

### Urban Context
```
Prompt pattern:
"Aerial/street view of [development type], [time of day], showing
[key features], urban context with [surrounding elements], architectural
visualization style, [weather/atmosphere]"

Example:
"Aerial view of mixed-use development at blue hour, showing activated
ground floor retail, residential towers above, urban context with
existing neighborhood, people on streets, architectural visualization style"
```

## Technical Specifications

### For Fal.ai Image Generation

```
Style keywords:
"architectural photography, 35mm lens, professional quality, editorial style,
real estate photography, luxury development, modern architecture, contemporary design,
[time of day] light, natural materials, human scale, urban context"

Negative prompt:
"fisheye lens, distorted perspective, over-saturated, HDR look, artificial colors,
empty sterile space, no human element, generic stock photo, cartoon, illustration,
3D render look, unrealistic lighting"

Quality settings:
- Natural color grading
- Visible material textures
- Proper architectural proportions
- Atmospheric depth where appropriate
- Realistic lighting and shadows
```

### Consistency Requirements
- Match time of day across sequence
- Consistent architectural style
- Same material palette
- Coherent human representation
- Uniform quality level

## What to Avoid

- Fisheye or extreme wide angle distortion
- Over-saturated "real estate marketing" colors
- Empty, sterile, unpopulated spaces
- Generic stock photography compositions
- 3D render appearance (too perfect)
- Neglecting material texture
- Forgetting human scale reference
- Poor lighting that flattens architecture
- Cluttered or confusing compositions
- Ignoring urban/landscape context
