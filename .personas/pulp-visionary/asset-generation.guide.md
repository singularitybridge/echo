# Asset Generation Guide: Pulp Visionary

## Visual Identity
Generate assets that evoke 1970s exploitation cinema, pulp fiction covers, and neo-noir aesthetics with bold colors and stylized realism.

## Character Design Principles

### Physical Characteristics
- **Realistic but stylized**: Grounded faces with cinematic quality
- **Distinctive features**: Strong jawlines, piercing eyes, memorable silhouettes
- **Period-appropriate styling**: 1970s-1990s fashion sensibilities
- **Cultural diversity**: Mix of ethnicities, each with authentic detail
- **Age variety**: From young criminals to weathered veterans

### Wardrobe & Styling
- **Classic cool**: Black suits, leather jackets, vintage tees
- **Retro patterns**: Loud shirts, bold colors, period-accurate cuts
- **Accessories**: Sunglasses, cigarettes (period-appropriate), jewelry
- **Hair**: Slicked back, afros, long hair, distinct styles
- **Footwear**: Boots, sneakers, dress shoes with character

### Color Palette for Characters
- High contrast: Deep blacks, bright whites
- Bold primaries: Red, yellow, blue in saturated tones
- Skin tones: Rich and warm, well-lit
- Accent colors: Gold, blood red, electric blue

## Environment & Background Design

### Settings
- **Diners**: Retro booths, neon signs, checkered floors
- **Urban streets**: Gritty but colorful, graffiti, vintage cars
- **Apartments**: Lived-in, cluttered, personality-filled
- **Bars/clubs**: Dark with colored lighting, smoke-filled
- **Cars**: Vintage American muscle cars, worn interiors

### Lighting Style
- Hard, directional light creating strong shadows
- Colored gels (amber, red, blue) for dramatic effect
- High contrast ratios
- Practical light sources visible (lamps, neons)
- Cinematic "shaft of light" effects

## Reference Image Generation

### Portrait References
- **Framing**: Medium close-ups, slight low angles for power
- **Expression**: Cool, confident, or intense stares
- **Lighting**: Three-point with hard shadows
- **Background**: Simple or environmental (diner booth, car interior)
- **Style**: Photorealistic with slight cinematic enhancement

### Landscape References
- **Composition**: Dynamic angles, dutch tilts welcome
- **Depth**: Multiple planes with strong foreground elements
- **Atmosphere**: Hazy, smoky, or high-contrast clear
- **Time of day**: Night scenes, golden hour, harsh midday
- **Urban texture**: Brick walls, neon signs, vintage details

## Props & Objects
- Vintage cars (1960s-1980s American)
- Retro diners with period-accurate details
- Guns (stylized but recognizable)
- Vintage suitcases, briefcases
- Period telephones, TVs, radios
- Alcohol bottles, cigarette packs
- Cash, playing cards, dice

## Artistic References
- Pulp fiction novel covers (1950s-1970s)
- Film noir cinematography
- Exploitation film posters
- Comic book art (especially crime comics)
- Robert McGinnis illustrations
- Saul Bass poster designs

## Technical Specifications

### For Fal.ai Image Generation
```
Style keywords:
"cinematic, pulp fiction aesthetic, 1970s crime film, high contrast lighting,
stylized realism, bold colors, film noir influence, retro cool"

Negative prompt:
"cartoon, anime, CGI, modern fashion, soft lighting, pastel colors,
clean aesthetic, corporate, sterile"

Quality settings:
- High detail, photorealistic base
- Strong lighting contrasts
- Saturated color grading
- Film grain texture acceptable
```

### Character Reference Consistency
- Maintain consistent facial features across all references
- Same wardrobe elements or clear costume changes
- Consistent lighting mood (even if different setups)
- Same time period indicators
- Recognizable character through different angles

## Mood & Atmosphere
- Cool and dangerous
- Lived-in and authentic
- Stylish without being clean
- Retro but not campy
- Intense but darkly humorous

## What to Avoid
- Modern, clean aesthetics
- Soft, diffused lighting
- Pastel or muted colors
- Generic stock photo looks
- Overly polished, CGI feel
- Fantasy or sci-fi elements
- Corporate or sterile environments
