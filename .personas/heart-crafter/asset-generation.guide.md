# Asset Generation Guide: Heart Crafter

## Visual Identity
Generate assets with vibrant colors, warm lighting, expressive characters, and magical realism that evokes wonder, emotion, and universal appeal.

## Character Design Principles

### Physical Characteristics
- **Expressive and appealing**: Warm, inviting faces with personality
- **Diverse representation**: All ages, ethnicities, body types with authenticity
- **Emotional transparency**: Faces that show feeling clearly
- **Approachable features**: Friendly eyes, genuine smiles, open expressions
- **Aspirational yet relatable**: Beautiful but grounded, not unrealistic

### Wardrobe & Styling
- **Colorful but coordinated**: Vibrant without being chaotic
- **Character-appropriate**: Clothing tells who they are
- **Practical with personality**: Functional but distinctive
- **Warm color tones**: Reds, oranges, yellows, warm blues
- **Layered with texture**: Knits, denim, cotton with visible detail
- **Accessories with meaning**: Items that reveal character

### Color Palette for Characters
- Vibrant primaries: Rich reds, bright yellows, deep blues
- Warm secondaries: Orange, coral, amber, teal
- Natural tones: Warm browns, olive greens, terracotta
- Skin tones: Warm and healthy, diverse range
- Hair: Full spectrum from black to platinum, rich colors

## Environment & Background Design

### Settings
- **Natural beauty**: Forests, mountains, oceans, magical landscapes
- **Cozy interiors**: Warm homes, inviting spaces, lived-in comfort
- **Whimsical locations**: Magical shops, fantastical villages, dream spaces
- **Emotional environments**: Settings that reflect character's journey
- **Cultural richness**: Authentic representations of diverse places
- **Golden hour magic**: Warm, glowing natural light

### Lighting Style
- Soft, warm, inviting
- Golden hour predominance
- Motivated but magical light sources
- Glowing, radiant quality
- Minimal harsh shadows
- Light that evokes emotion
- Warm color temperature

## Reference Image Generation

### Portrait References
- **Framing**: Medium shots, slightly off-center for dynamism
- **Expression**: Warm, genuine emotion (joy, determination, wonder)
- **Lighting**: Soft, warm, three-point with fill
- **Background**: Beautiful but not distracting, supports character
- **Style**: Painterly realism with warmth

### Landscape References
- **Composition**: Rule of thirds, dynamic but balanced
- **Depth**: Multiple layers, atmospheric perspective
- **Color**: Vibrant but harmonious palette
- **Scale**: Epic vistas that inspire wonder
- **Perspective**: Sweeping, inviting, adventurous

## Mood & Atmosphere
- Warm and inviting
- Hopeful and optimistic
- Emotionally resonant
- Magical but grounded
- Wonder and discovery
- Safe yet adventurous
- Heartfelt sincerity

## Environmental Details
- Lush natural environments
- Warm, lived-in interiors
- Magic in everyday details
- Cultural authenticity
- Seasonal beauty (autumn leaves, spring blooms)
- Weather as mood (gentle rain, sunshine, snow)
- Scale that inspires wonder

## Color Harmony Principles
- Analogous color schemes (harmonious)
- Warm dominant palette
- Complementary accents for visual interest
- Saturation for emotional beats
- Color psychology: warm = safe, cool = mystery
- Gradient skies (sunset oranges to purples)
- Natural color relationships

## Emotional Visual Cues
- **Warmth through color**: Oranges, golds, warm light
- **Safety through framing**: Cozy, protected spaces
- **Wonder through scale**: Vast landscapes, tiny characters
- **Joy through vibrancy**: Saturated, happy colors
- **Growth through nature**: Plants, seasons, life cycles
- **Connection through composition**: Characters together, touching

## Magical Realism Elements
- Sparkles and light particles
- Glowing objects or creatures
- Slightly impossible physics (floating, etc.)
- Anthropomorphic nature (expressive trees, clouds)
- Dream-like color intensity
- Subtle fantasy elements in real settings
- Light as magical force

## Cinematographic Techniques
- Wide establishing shots showing beautiful worlds
- Close-ups for emotional connection
- Crane/sweeping camera moves (implied)
- Dynamic angles that create energy
- Depth of field for focus on emotion
- Lens flares and light effects for magic

## What to Avoid
- Dark, desaturated color palettes
- Cold or harsh lighting
- Cynical or scary imagery
- Sterile, minimal environments
- Realistic violence or darkness
- Muted or sad color schemes
- Isolated, lonely framing
- Gritty realism without hope
- Modern urban decay
- Anything that creates fear or sadness
