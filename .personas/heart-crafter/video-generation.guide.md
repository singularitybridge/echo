# Video Generation Guide: Heart Crafter

## Core Cinematic Principles
Generate videos with emotional resonance, dynamic camera work, vibrant colors, and heartwarming aesthetics that inspire wonder and connection.

## Camera Work & Movement

### Signature Shots
- **Sweeping crane shots**: Graceful upward or circular reveals
- **Dynamic dolly moves**: Smooth tracking with characters
- **Whip pans with motivation**: Quick transitions with energy
- **Push-ins on emotion**: Moving closer during key moments
- **Orbiting movements**: Circular camera around subjects
- **Establishing aerials**: Wide beauty shots showing scale

### Camera Movement Style
- **Fluid and expressive**: Camera dances with story
- **Motivated by emotion**: Moves reflect feeling
- **Energetic but controlled**: Dynamic without chaos
- **Revealing wonder**: Camera discovers beautiful elements
- **Following characters**: Tracking alongside journey
- **Rising with hope**: Upward movements for inspiration

## Scene Composition

### Framing Principles
- **Rule of thirds**: Classic balanced compositions
- **Leading lines**: Paths, roads, natural elements guide eye
- **Depth and layers**: Multiple planes of visual interest
- **Character-focused**: Subjects clearly defined in frame
- **Environmental context**: Beautiful backgrounds matter
- **Dynamic staging**: Characters moving through space

### Visual Style
- **Vibrant saturation**: Rich, beautiful colors throughout
- **Warm golden lighting**: Inviting, hopeful illumination
- **Magical atmosphere**: Subtle wonder in reality
- **Natural beauty**: Gorgeous landscapes and environments
- **Expressive staging**: Body language and position tell story
- **Detailed environments**: Worlds worth exploring

## Pacing & Rhythm

### Timing
- **Emotional beats**: 8 seconds perfect for feeling moments
- **Varied pacing**: Quiet reflection to energetic action
- **Comedic timing**: Well-timed visual gags
- **Musical rhythm**: Movements can sync to implied music
- **Breathing room**: Allow moments to land

### Energy Levels
- **Medium to high**: Engaging and alive
- **Emotional range**: Joy, sadness, wonder, determination
- **Uplifting arc**: Even sad moments have hope
- **Dynamic variation**: Energy ebbs and flows

## Lighting Approach

### Light Quality
- **Warm and inviting**: Golden hour aesthetic
- **Soft but defined**: Gentle shadows with detail
- **Magical quality**: Light has emotional color
- **Motivatedfrom nature**: Sun, fire, practical sources
- **Glowing highlights**: Rim light, backlighting for beauty
- **Rich saturation**: Colors enhanced by lighting

### Lighting Scenarios
- **Golden hour**: Warm sunlight, long shadows, magic
- **Interior warmth**: Fireplace, lamps, cozy glowing
- **Magical night**: Moonlight, lanterns, starlight
- **Bright day**: Clear skies, vibrant natural light
- **Dramatic moments**: Storm light, sunset, dawn

## Editing & Transitions

### Cut Style
- **Emotional timing**: Cuts on feeling beats
- **Dynamic pacing**: Varied rhythm, never monotonous
- **Montage sequences**: Time passage with visual storytelling
- **Whip transitions**: Energetic scene changes
- **Dissolves for emotion**: Soft transitions for touching moments

### Montage Techniques
- **Growth montages**: Character developing over time
- **Journey sequences**: Travel and discovery
- **Relationship building**: Connection developing visually
- **Musical sequences**: Action choreographed to rhythm
- **Emotional crescendos**: Building to feeling peaks

## Prompt Construction for Veo 3.1

### Essential Elements
```
[BEAUTIFUL DESCRIPTION] + [DYNAMIC CAMERA] + [WARM LIGHTING] + [STYLE KEYWORDS]

Example:
"Child runs through sunlit meadow filled with wildflowers, arms spread wide
with joy. Sweeping crane shot following and rising. Warm golden hour
lighting, vibrant saturated colors. Pixar style, heartwarming, magical
realism, beautiful environment, emotional, cinematic."
```

### Style Keywords to Include
- "Pixar style" or "Studio Ghibli aesthetic"
- "Warm golden lighting, vibrant colors"
- "Heartwarming, emotional"
- "Beautiful environment, rich detail"
- "Magical realism"
- "Expressive character animation"
- "Sweeping camera movement" (or other dynamic move)
- "Inspiring, hopeful"

### Camera Move Specifications
- "Sweeping crane shot, rising and revealing"
- "Dynamic dolly tracking with character"
- "Smooth circular orbit around subject"
- "Gentle push-in on emotional moment"
- "Whip pan transition with energy"
- "Following alongside character's journey"

## Dialogue Integration

### Voiceover Formatting
```
Character says, "Heartfelt dialogue expressing genuine emotion" (no
subtitles). Camera pushes in during delivery for intimacy.
```

### Dialogue Scene Techniques
- Keep voiceover 12-25 words for 8-second clips
- Specify emotional camera movement
- Include beautiful environmental context
- Note warm, vibrant lighting

## Emotion & Wonder

### Heartwarming Elements
- **Genuine smiles and laughter**: Authentic joy
- **Touching connections**: Characters connecting physically/emotionally
- **Beautiful natural moments**: Sunlight, wildlife, seasons
- **Achievement and growth**: Characters succeeding, learning
- **Found family**: Group togetherness
- **Small magical touches**: Subtle fantasy elements

### Emotional Beats
- "Character realizes truth, tears in eyes"
- "Heartfelt embrace, warm backlighting"
- "Triumphant moment, camera rising"
- "Quiet reflection, gentle push-in"
- "Joyful discovery, dynamic reveal"
- "Bittersweet goodbye, soft lighting"

## Environmental Integration

### Location Specifics
- **Natural beauty**: "Lush forest with dappled sunlight, magical atmosphere"
- **Cozy interiors**: "Warm home, fireplace glowing, family gathered"
- **Magical locations**: "Hidden waterfall, rainbow mist, ethereal light"
- **Cultural richness**: "Vibrant market, colorful fabrics, bustling life"
- **Epic vistas**: "Mountain range at sunset, vast and inspiring"

## Color & Mood

### Color Specifications
- "Vibrant saturated colors, rich and beautiful"
- "Warm golden lighting, inviting glow"
- "Analogous color harmony, pleasing palette"
- "Natural colors enhanced, not muted"
- "Gradient skies, sunset oranges to purples"
- "Complementary accents for visual interest"

### Mood Descriptors
- "Heartwarming and hopeful"
- "Joyful and inspiring"
- "Emotionally resonant"
- "Magical and wonderful"
- "Tender and sincere"
- "Triumphant and uplifting"

## Technical Specifications

### Video Settings Recommendations
- **Resolution**: 1080p (for beautiful color and detail)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 landscape (classic cinematic) or 9:16 portrait (intimate character focus)
- **Duration**: 8 seconds (perfect for emotional beats)
- **Looping**: Sometimes yes (for magical or joyful moments)

### Reference Image Usage
- Use character references with warm, vibrant lighting
- Ensure reference shows expressive, genuine emotion
- Previous shot for emotional continuity in journey
- Maintain consistent heartwarming tone throughout

## Example Prompts

### Joyful Discovery
```
"Young girl discovers hidden garden filled with glowing fireflies at dusk,
eyes wide with wonder. A girl says, 'I've never seen anything so beautiful'
(no subtitles). Sweeping crane shot rising to reveal garden's full magic.
Warm golden hour light, vibrant greens and glowing yellows. Pixar style,
heartwarming, magical realism, beautiful environment, emotional wonder,
cinematic. Medium to wide shot."
```

### Heartfelt Connection
```
"Parent and child embrace on beach at sunset, waves gently in background.
Slow push-in on their faces, genuine emotion. Warm orange and pink sunset
lighting, rich golden tones, soft backlight. Heartwarming, Pixar aesthetic,
tender moment, emotional sincerity, beautiful natural environment, cinematic.
Medium close-up."
```

### Triumphant Moment
```
"Character reaches mountain summit, raises arms in triumph as sun rises. Wide
establishing aerial shot, sweeping crane rising with character. Warm sunrise
lighting, vibrant orange and gold skies, rich saturated colors. Inspiring,
Pixar style, triumphant achievement, epic scale, heartwarming, cinematic.
Wide aerial shot."
```

### Cozy Interior Warmth
```
"Family gathered around dinner table, laughing together, fireplace glowing in
background. Gentle circular orbit around table. Warm interior lighting, rich
amber tones, inviting atmosphere. Heartwarming, Pixar aesthetic, found
family, cozy home, emotional connection, beautiful detail, cinematic. Medium
wide shot."
```

### Magical Nature
```
"Child runs through meadow of wildflowers, butterflies rising around them in
slow motion. Dynamic tracking alongside, celebrating movement. Warm golden
hour light, vibrant flower colors, magical particles in air. Studio Ghibli
aesthetic, heartwarming, magical realism, beautiful nature, joyful, cinematic.
Medium tracking shot."
```

## What to Avoid
- Dark, desaturated color palettes
- Cold or harsh lighting
- Static, locked-off shots (use sparingly)
- Cynical or scary content
- Gritty realism without hope
- Isolated, lonely framing
- Muted or sad color schemes
- Sterile environments
- Uncomfortable or disturbing imagery
- Anything that creates fear or sadness without hope
