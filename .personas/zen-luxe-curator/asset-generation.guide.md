# Asset Generation Guide: Zen Luxe Curator

## Visual Identity
Generate assets with serene Japanese aesthetics, natural beauty, warm hospitality moments, and the understated elegance of luxury that whispers rather than shouts.

## The 1898 Niseko Visual Language

### Core Aesthetic
- **Wabi-sabi beauty**: Imperfection, patina, natural aging
- **Minimalist elegance**: Negative space, clean lines, purposeful restraint
- **Natural materials**: Wood grain, stone texture, woven fabrics, paper
- **Seasonal sensitivity**: Snow, blossoms, green leaves, autumn colors
- **Warm humanity**: Staff warmth, guest comfort, genuine moments

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors
- **Snow White** (#F8F9FA): Fresh powder, clean linens, steam
- **Warm Amber** (#D4A574): Interior lighting, sake, firelight
- **Forest Green** (#2D4A3E): Pine trees, tatami edges, nature
- **Deep Indigo** (#1E3A5F): Evening sky, traditional textiles
- **Natural Wood** (#8B7355): Cypress, cedar, architectural elements

#### Accent Colors
- **Hot Spring Blue** (#7FB3D3): Onsen water, sky reflections
- **Autumn Red** (#B85C38): Maple leaves, sunset, kaiseki accents
- **Sake Gold** (#C9A962): Premium touches, lighting warmth
- **Volcanic Black** (#2C2C2C): Rock, contrast elements, grounding

#### Seasonal Palettes
- **Winter**: Whites, deep blues, amber warmth, evergreen
- **Spring**: Soft pinks, fresh greens, clear sky blue
- **Summer**: Lush greens, sky blues, earth tones
- **Autumn**: Reds, oranges, golds, warm browns

## Lighting Approach

### Natural Light
- **Golden hour**: Warm sunset on snow, long shadows
- **Blue hour**: Twilight magic, interior warmth contrast
- **Soft overcast**: Even, flattering, emphasizes textures
- **Bright snow**: Reflected light, pristine brightness

### Interior Light
- **Warm ambient**: Fire glow, paper lanterns, indirect lighting
- **Focused accent**: Highlighting food, art, architectural details
- **Soft overall**: Never harsh, always inviting
- **Layered depth**: Multiple light sources for dimension

### Onsen & Steam
- **Backlit steam**: Dramatic atmosphere
- **Warm water glow**: Reflections and movement
- **Contrast with cold**: Steam in cold air, indoor/outdoor

## Compositional Guidelines

### Japanese Aesthetic Principles
- **Ma (negative space)**: Allow breathing room
- **Asymmetrical balance**: Natural, not rigid
- **Layered depth**: Foreground, middle, background
- **Frame within frame**: Doorways, windows, screens
- **Leading elements**: Natural paths for the eye

### Camera Positions
- **Guest perspective**: What they see arriving, entering
- **Environmental context**: Show space and setting
- **Intimate detail**: Close-ups of quality and craft
- **Elevated views**: Mountain panoramas, roof lines
- **Ground level**: Snow texture, path approaching

## Seasonal Considerations

### Winter (Primary Focus)
- Fresh powder snow
- Steam against cold air
- Warm interior glow visible outside
- Ski equipment and gear
- Hot drinks and warming food
- Fireplace scenes

### Other Seasons
- Spring: Cherry blossoms, melting snow, renewal
- Summer: Lush greens, hiking, cool mountain air
- Autumn: Red and gold foliage, harvest foods

## What to Avoid

- Generic stock hotel imagery
- Overcrowded or cluttered scenes
- Cold, sterile modern aesthetics
- Harsh or unflattering lighting
- Cultural inauthenticity or stereotypes
- Cheap materials or budget feel
- Over-saturated or artificial colors
- Posed or stiff human subjects
- Missing environmental context
- Inconsistent visual quality
